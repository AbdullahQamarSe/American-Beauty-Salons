FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first visit?
On your first visit, you'll be greeted with a warm welcome and a complimentary beverage. We'll start with a thorough consultation to understand your beauty goals, hair/skin type, and preferences before recommending the best treatments tailored just for you.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee of 50% of the service cost. We understand emergencies happen, so please call us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
Do you offer bridal or group packages?
Absolutely! We specialize in bridal beauty and group bookings. Our bridal packages include trials, day-of styling, makeup, and exclusive use of our private suite. Contact us for custom group quotes for parties of 3 or more.
Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?
Yes! We exclusively use cruelty-free, ethically sourced products. Many of our product lines are also vegan and organic. We're proud to partner with brands that share our commitment to sustainability and animal welfare.
Is parking available at your location?
Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all clients at 100 William Street. Simply pull up to our entrance and our valet team will take care of the rest. Public transit is also convenient with multiple subway lines nearby.
